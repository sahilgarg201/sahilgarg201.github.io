GST CONNECT

CONNECTED BANKING

Banking is one of the mainstay of any ERP use . We have made it connected with ICICI bank . Account holders of this bank , now need not do transactions on bank website or app. Now make payments, fetch receipts, even month end reconciliation can be done in a jiffy. Save on manpower cost, errors and mismatch headaches

1ERP

Today money transactions happen from one user to another in seconds. We therefore envision to implement a realtime transaction platform , where the seller invoice be notified to its buyer and a payment note from buyer be sent to seller as receipt in real time. That too irrespective of the user ERP. The objective is to make transactions paperless and create a dmat format of trading for all businesses.

OCR PURCAHSE

A revolutionary concept of purchase transaction entry in Tally is hereby unfolded. Scan any computer generated soft/hard copy of purchase invoice ,extract important data from pdf and get it updated on Tally as purchase voucher. This is a premium service and is available for dedicated premium subscribers only.
